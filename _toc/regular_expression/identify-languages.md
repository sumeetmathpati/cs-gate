### 1) $L = \\{wxw^R \| w \in (a, b)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Not regular.

### 2) $L = \\{wxw^R \| w \in (a)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Not regular.

### 3) $L = \\{ww^R \| w \in (a)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Regular.
	- $L = (aa)^*$

### 6) $L = \\{ww^Rx \| w, x \in (a, b)^+\\}$

- **Answer:** Not regular.

### 7) $L = \\{wxw^R \| w, x \in (a, b)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Regular.
	- $L = (a+b)^*$

### 8) $L = \\{ww \| w \in (a)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Regular.
	- $L = (aa)^*$

### 9) $L = \\{ww \| w \in (a, b)^*\\}$

- **Answer:** Not regular.

### 10) $L = \\{a^nb^n \| n >= 1\\}$

- **Answer:** Not regular.

### 9) $L = \\{a^nb^n \| 1 <= n <= 10000\\}$

- **Answer:** Regular.
	- Because language is finite.
