### Ansnwer: **D**

### Explaination:

- Let $\Sigma=\{a,b\}.$.
- $L_1 = \Sigma^*$ is a regular language
- $L_2 = \{ww^R \mid w \in (a+b)^*\}$ is a context free language. 
- $L_1\cap L_2 = \{ww^R \mid w \in (a+b)^*\}$ which is clearly context free language and not DCFL or Regular
- Hence, the answer is option D.
